## Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (Adapted for NXTG‑Forge)

### Our Pledge  
We, the maintainers and contributors of **NXTG‑Forge**, pledge to create a welcoming, safe, and productive environment for everyone.  
We are committed to building reproducible, transparent, and empowering developer tools — and that starts with how we treat each other.

### Our Standards  
Examples of behavior that fosters a positive environment:

- Clear, constructive communication  
- Respect for different viewpoints and experiences  
- Graceful acceptance of feedback  
- Focus on reproducible, actionable contributions  
- Empathy toward other community members  
- Commitment to documentation and clarity for future contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Dismissive or hostile responses to questions or feedback  
- Publishing others’ private information  
- Disruptive or intentionally misleading contributions  
- Knowingly introducing instability, ambiguity, or unsafe changes

### Our Responsibilities  
Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing contributions fairly and consistently  
- Taking appropriate corrective action when behavior is unacceptable  
- Maintaining the integrity, safety, and reproducibility of the project

### Scope  
This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, docs, commits)  
- Any public or private communication related to the project  
- Any interaction where a contributor represents the project or community

### Enforcement  
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at:

**axw@nxtg.ai**

All complaints will be reviewed and handled fairly, consistently, and respectfully.

### Enforcement Guidelines  
Maintainers may take actions including:

1. **Correction** — A private, clear explanation of the issue  
2. **Warning** — A formal notice of unacceptable behavior  
3. **Temporary Ban** — Removal from project spaces for a defined period  
4. **Permanent Ban** — Removal from all project spaces and contributions
